Hue Remote for Watch

Control Hue lights from Apple Watch.

A native, independent watchOS app for controlling a Philips Hue Bridge from your wrist.

Support Privacy Policy

Current Features

  • Find and pair a Hue Bridge on the local network.
  • Add a bridge manually by IP address.
  • Control rooms, zones, and individual lights.
  • Turn lights on or off and adjust brightness.
  • Choose color presets, custom colors, and saved quick colors.
  • Use Finnish or English, following the Apple Watch language by default.
  • Add and forget paired bridges.
  • Refresh saved bridge addresses when the local network route changes.
