Hue Remote for Apple Watch

Control your Hue lights from your Apple Watch, wherever you are.

A native Apple Watch app with an iPhone companion for setup, synchronization, and secure remote access.

Support Privacy Policy

Current Features

  • Find, pair, select, and remove multiple Hue Bridges.
  • Set up a bridge from your iPhone or Apple Watch, or add it manually using its IP address.
  • Control rooms, zones, entertainment areas, and individual lights.
  • Turn supported lights on or off and adjust their brightness.
  • Choose presets, adjust colors in real time, and save favorite colors for quick access.
  • Rename or delete your saved colors.
  • Arrange rooms and the lights inside each room.
  • Connect your Hue account to control lights outside your home network.
  • Securely synchronize bridge credentials and remote-access sessions with your Apple Watch.
  • The app is available in English and Finnish.
