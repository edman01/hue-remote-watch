Hue Remote for Watch

Control Hue lights from Apple Watch, wherever you are.

A native Apple Watch controller with an iPhone companion for setup, synchronization, and secure remote access.

Support Privacy Policy

Current Features

  • Find, pair, select, and remove multiple Hue Bridges.
  • Set up a bridge from iPhone, Apple Watch, or a manual IP address.
  • Control rooms, zones, entertainment areas, and individual lights.
  • Use on/off controls and brightness for supported lights.
  • Choose presets, adjust live custom colors, and save quick colors.
  • Rename or delete saved quick colors.
  • Arrange rooms and the lights inside each room.
  • Connect a Hue account for control outside the home network.
  • Synchronize bridge and remote-access sessions securely to Apple Watch.
  • Use Finnish or English.
