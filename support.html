Hue Remote for Watch

Setup and troubleshooting for the iPhone and Apple Watch apps.

Getting Started

  1. Connect iPhone or Apple Watch to the same Wi-Fi network as the Hue Bridge.
  2. Find and select the bridge, press its large round link button, then choose Connect bridge.
  3. Open Hue Remote on Apple Watch and synchronize from the iPhone companion app.
  4. Sign in to a Hue account and synchronize again to enable control outside the home network.

Troubleshooting

If a bridge is not found, confirm local-network permission and try its IP address manually. Keep the Watch app open while synchronizing. Remote control requires a connected Hue account and an internet connection.
