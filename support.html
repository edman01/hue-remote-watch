Hue Remote for Watch

A native Apple Watch app for controlling Hue lights through your Hue Bridge.

Supported Features

  • Bridge discovery on the local network.
  • Manual bridge setup by IP address.
  • Bridge pairing with the physical link button.
  • Rooms, zones, and individual light control.
  • On/off, brightness, color presets, custom colors, and quick colors.
  • Multiple paired bridges with the option to forget a bridge.
  • Saved bridge address refresh when the local network route changes.
  • Finnish and English language support.
