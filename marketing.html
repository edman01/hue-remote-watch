Simple setup

Start on iPhone. Continue on your wrist.

  1. Connect your bridge. Find a Hue Bridge on your local network and press its round link button to pair.
  2. Connect your Hue account when needed. Enable secure control outside your home network.
  3. Synchronize with Apple Watch. Bridge and remote-access information are transferred together.
Requirements

Designed for a Hue Bridge home.

Hue Remote requires a compatible Hue Bridge. An iPhone is used for companion setup and optional Hue account sign-in. Remote control requires an internet connection and a connected Hue account.

The app is available in English and Finnish.
