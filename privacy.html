Privacy Policy

Hue Remote for Watch

Last updated: July 4, 2026

Data Collection

Hue Remote does not collect, sell, or share personal data.

Local Network Use

The app uses the local network to find and control your Hue Bridge. Bridge credentials are stored securely on your Apple Watch using Keychain.

App Data Stored on Device

The app may store settings such as language preference, room order, paired bridge information, and saved quick colors on your device.

Analytics and Tracking

The app does not use third-party analytics, advertising SDKs, or tracking technologies.
